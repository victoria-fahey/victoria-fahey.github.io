Emotional Intelligence



What is emotional intelligence?


Emotional intelligence is the ability to monitor, manage and handle one’s own emotion. It also encompasses the ability to perceive and be aware of other people’s emotions also.


How is it different to IQ?


IQ is the measure or assessment of one’s ability to solve problems, use logic and understand complex ideas. This is different to emotional intelligence as IQ only takes into account the technical side of things, whereas emotional intelligence uses awareness of emotions to guide your decisions.


Why is it important?


Emotional intelligence is important as it allows you to empathises with others, communicate effectively, and be both self and socially aware. This can influence your job performance, relationships and overall well-being.
